Meet Our Master Barbers

Mark - Owner & Master Barber

With over 15 years of experience behind the chair, Mark has built White Swan Barber Shop into Fort Wayne's premier destination for classic barbering. His dedication to the craft and commitment to each client's satisfaction has earned him a loyal following throughout the community.

Mark specializes in timeless cuts, precision fades, and traditional shaves, combining old-school techniques with modern styling sensibilities. His friendly demeanor and professional expertise create an atmosphere where every client feels valued and leaves looking their absolute best.

Logan - Master Barber

Logan brings exceptional skill and attention to detail to every cut. Known for his precision work on modern fades, classic gentleman's cuts, and meticulous beard grooming, Logan has developed a reputation for consistency and craftsmanship since joining the White Swan team.

With a passion for both traditional barbering techniques and contemporary styling, Logan excels at helping clients achieve their ideal look while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and customer service that White Swan is known for.
